Method Overview

MambaHSISR is a Mamba-based framework for single hyperspectral image super-resolution. The framework uses a spatial Mamba subnetwork to model long-range dependencies in pixel space, a spectral Mamba subnetwork to capture long-range spectral dependencies, and a reconstruction component to generate high-resolution hyperspectral images with spatial and spectral details. The paper reports experiments on the Houston and QUST-1 datasets.
